Adshel is our national network of bus stop 6-sheet panels located in key cities, communities and on high streets.

Think the box outside / Reach / Planning Tools & Insight / Innovation / Creativity / Infographics

Think the box outside

When you think about national coverage, impact and engagement in out-of-home advertising, nothing delivers like Adshel. It provides a big canvas for big creative ideas.

Our 45,000 national Adshel panels are situated in premium locations throughout the country, allowing advertisers to target audiences on a local and national scale.

Adshel has helped brands meet people for the last 25 years and it has developed to meet the evolving needs of advertisers and consumers.

Reach

A national Adshel campaign can reach more people than any other out-of-home advertising format. It enables advertisers to create a unique and consistent out-of-home footprint nationwide and delivers up to half a billion impacts, or 74% coverage of the UK adult population, in just two weeks. So your big idea will get an even bigger audience.

Adshel also has the power to reach specific people in targeted locations at the right times.

Planning tools & insight

Adshel campaigns lets brands reach the right people at the right moments in the most relevant places. Our Insight team has a deep understanding of advertisers’ audiences, including their lifestyle and behaviours, which helps brands target their desired audiences. This is achieved through our unique planning tools and analysis of Route data.

Clear Channel’s Campaign Planning team work tirelessly to develop the perfect campaigns for our partners which helps them achieve their goals.

 

Innovation

Adshel is continuously innovating, keeping it as relevant today as it has ever been.

We recently introduced our landmark Mobile Platform which incorporates Near Field Communication (NFC) and QR code technologies onto 10,000 Adshel panels nationwide. Brands can now offer interaction, social media integration and real-time content updates as part of their Adshel campaigns.

But our innovation extends to how we partner with brands. Our planning tools and industry-leading proof-of-posting systems give advertisers confidence that their Adshel campaigns are well planned and executed.

Our continued investment into energy efficient technologies, including LED lighting, means we deliver more impactful advertising and ensure Clear Channel continues to be increasingly sustainable.

Creativity

Adshel has provided the perfect creative canvas to brands for more than a quarter of a century. It allows our partners to harness the power of impactful creative in engaging audiences.

In addition, our award-winning Create team has a long history of working in partnership with advertisers to push the creative boundaries of out-of-home and bringing ideas to life.

This can be achieved through a simple creative idea or harnessing the power of technology – the result is always a memorable and innovative out-of-home campaign.
